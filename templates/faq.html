Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using NOVUS, uploading content, and managing your library.

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Click the "Register" button in the top right corner of the login page. Fill in your username, email, and password. You can choose to register as a Reader (to access and read content) or as a Publisher (to upload and manage books/manga). Once registered, log in to start exploring!

What's the difference between Reader and Publisher roles?

Readers can browse, read books, and manage their watchlist. Publishers can do everything Readers can, plus upload new books and manga, edit their uploads, manage chapters, and track their reader engagement.

Can I change my role after registration?

Contact the admin team through the Help & Info menu if you wish to upgrade your role from Reader to Publisher or have other role-related requests.

Using the Library

How do I search for books?

Use the search bar on the dashboard to search by title or author. You can also filter by category using the category dropdown. Search results update instantly as you type.

What's the Watchlist feature?

Your Watchlist is a personal collection where you can track books you want to read, are currently reading, or have finished. Click the bookmark icon on any book to add it to your watchlist. You can organize books by status (Planned, Reading, On Hold, Dropped) and set your reading progress percentage. Use the search and filter buttons to quickly find books in your watchlist.

Can I download books offline?

PDF files can be downloaded and read in any PDF reader. Audio files can be streamed or downloaded depending on your device. Check individual book pages for download options.

Publishing Content

How do I upload a book or manga?

As a Publisher, go to "Add New Content" in the navigation menu. Choose either Book or Manga, fill in the title, author, category, and description. Upload a PDF file and optionally an audio file (for books) or first chapter PDF (for manga). Add a cover image and click "Publish to Catalog" or "Create Manga Series".

What file formats are supported?

Documents: PDF (up to 50MB) for books and manga chapters.
Audio: MP3 (up to 100MB) for audiobook narration.
Images: JPG or PNG (up to 5MB) for cover images.

How do I add chapters to my manga?

Go to your profile or "My Uploads" page and find the manga series. Click "Upload Chapter" to add a new chapter PDF. Each chapter should be a complete PDF file with numbered pages.

Can I edit my uploaded content?

Yes! Go to "My Uploads" to see all your books and manga. Click "Edit" on any title to update the description, category, cover image, or other details. You can also delete content if needed.

Content Categories

What categories are available?

For Books: Action, Adventure, Children, Comedy, Drama, Fantasy, General, Historical, Horror, Mystery, Poetry, Romance, Science Fiction, Supernatural, Thriller, Young Adult.

For Manga: Action, Adventure, Comedy, Drama, Fantasy, Historical, Horror, Medical, Music, Post-Apocalyptic, Psychological, Romance, School, Sci-Fi, Slice of Life, Sports, Supernatural.

Choose the category that best fits your content when uploading.

Can I change a book's category after uploading?

Yes! Go to "My Uploads", click "Edit" on the book, and update the category field. Save your changes and the category will be updated immediately.

Account & Profile

How do I view my profile?

Click your username in the top-right navigation bar to access your profile. Here you can see your reading history, currently reading books, and uploaded content (if you're a Publisher).

How do I reset my password?

Password reset functionality is currently being developed. For now, contact the admin team through Help & Info for password assistance.

Can I delete my account?

Account deletion requests can be submitted through Help & Info. This action is permanent and will remove all your data from the system.

Didn't find your answer?

If you have a question not covered here, feel free to reach out through the Help & Info menu for more personalized assistance.
